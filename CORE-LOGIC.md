# 🧒 EduKid AI - Core Logic

## 🎯 Vision
"Eliminate parenting mental load through automated AI-driven routines and reminders."

## 📅 Age-Based Routine Logic

### 0–3 months (Newborn)
- ⏰ 7:00 AM: Wake & Feed
- 9:00 AM: Diaper change + bonding
- 11:00 AM: Feed
- 1:00 PM: Nap (1–2h)
- 3:00 PM: Feed
- 5:00 PM: Sensory play
- 7:00 PM: Feed + bedtime
- 8:00 PM: Sleep

### 4–6 months (Early stimulation)
- 7:00 AM: Wake & Feed
- 8:30 AM: Tummy time (5–10 min)
- 10:00 AM: Feed
- 11:00 AM: Motor skills play
- 1:00 PM: Nap (1–2h)
- 3:00 PM: Feed
- 4:00 PM: Sensory play
- 6:00 PM: Feed
- 7:30 PM: Sleep

### 7–12 months (Crawler)
- 7:00 AM: Wake & Feed (solids + fluids)
- 8:30 AM: Crawling practice
- 10:00 AM: Meal (mashed fruits/veg)
- 11:00 AM: Babbling + sign language
- 1:00 PM: Nap (1–2h)
- 3:00 PM: Meal
- 4:00 PM: Play with toys
- 7:30 PM: Sleep

### 1–2 years (Toddler)
- 7:00 AM: Wake + Breakfast
- 8:30 AM: Playtime (blocks, puzzles)
- 10:00 AM: Snack + Outdoor
- 12:00 PM: Lunch
- 1:00 PM: Nap (1h)
- 3:00 PM: Learning (letters, numbers)
- 5:30 PM: Quiet play (books)
- 7:00 PM: Dinner
- 8:00 PM: Sleep

### 3–5 years (Preschooler)
- 7:00 AM: Wake + Breakfast
- 8:30 AM: Learning sessions
- 10:30 AM: Social games
- 12:00 PM: Lunch
- 1:00 PM: Outside play
- 3:30 PM: Creative activities
- 6:00 PM: Dinner + Family talk
- 8:00 PM: Sleep + Storytime

### 6–10 years (School age)
- 6:30 AM: Wake + Breakfast
- 7:30 AM: School / Study
- 2:00 PM: Snack + Homework
- 4:00 PM: Physical activity
- 6:00 PM: Dinner
- 7:30 PM: Study/Reading
- 9:00 PM: Sleep

## ⏰ Reminder System

### Notification Examples

**0–3 months:**
- "Abhi child ko khana khilaye"
- "Sleep time ho gaya hai"
- "Diaper change karein"

**4–12 months:**
- "Abhi child ko solid khana khilaye"
- "Tummy time session karein"
- "Crawling practice encourage karein"

**1–5 years:**
- "Abhi child ko breakfast khilaye"
- "Play time start karein"
- "Study time shuru karein"

**6–10 years:**
- "Study time shuru karein"
- "Screen time baandho"

**Each notification includes:**
- Clear WHAT to do
- Explanatory HOW to do it
- Age-specific tip (from curated lists, optionally AI supplemented)

## 🔮 Future AI Logic (Phase 3+)

**Default Mode (Most efficient, local lists):** Expert-curated static age lists + parental tips

**Optional AI Enhancements (Opt-in):**
- Toggle lock icon in settings
- OpenAI integration variant suggestions when opted in

**Philosophy:** Core application works entirely offline via curated lists. AI features are purely opt-in supplements, not requirements.

---

## 🎯 Design Principles

- ⚡ ZERO clicks for core actions
- 🌐 Hinglish natural language throughout UI and notifications
- 👶 Clear morning → evening visual timeline
- 🔔 Reminder prominence (don’t miss key times)
- 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Focus on child, not app complexity
